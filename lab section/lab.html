Services Offered

We have successfully established the following sections of testing:

  • Core Laboratory
      Complete automation, Biochemistry, Hematology and clinical pathology analyzers
  • Hemoglobinopathy Laboratory
      HPLCs and Capillary Electrophoresis for Hemoglobinopathy diagnosis
  • Cytogenetics & Molecular Diagnostics Laboratory
      Karyotyping and Fluorescent In Situ Hybridisation assays, BOB’s
  • Microarray Lab
      Affymetrix platform along with PCR and sequencing services.
  • Bio analytical Laboratory
      HPLC, HPTLC, LCMS, GCMS, ICPMS,AAA for bioanalytical analysis and new born screening
  • Clinical Research Unit
      Supports contracted Clinical Research across the Hospitals of the group.
      It also supports external and internal funded student and faculty Research
  • independent quality assurance team
      Responsible for maintenance of QMS, Is also responsible for laboratory Quality Management Systems and carries out Interlab comparisons across the group hospitals
